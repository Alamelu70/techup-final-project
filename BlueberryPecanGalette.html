Tips

  • The dough is hardest to master. You need to make sure that all your ingredients are cold. Sometimes, after preparing the dough, I stick it back in the fridge to ensure that everything chills again.
  • Since you are going to the trouble of toasting the pecans and preparing the dough, I would recommend doubling the recipe and keeping the second portion in your freezer for use another time. It lasts a month.
