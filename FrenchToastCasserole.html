French Toast Casserole

This recipe is courtesy of the site, The Kitchn.com. I decided to give this a try for a Christmas brunch one year and it was a huge hit. The only thing I changed from the original recipe was I used double the amount of crumble topping (hello, this requires no explanations right?). I served the casserole with warm maple syrup. Christmas mornings never tasted better.

Recipe is linked here

Tips

  • This dish should be prepared the night before. Prepare the bread, the custard mixture, mix and let it refrigerate overnight
  • Prepare the crumble topping and keep it separately
  • You MUST use sourdough bread for this recipe. The crustiness of the sourdough is the chef's kiss in this recipe.
