Laksa Goreng

Before purists come at me, this is a very personalised laksa goreng, not at all authentic. First it is vegetarian. Second, I cook it with untraditional ingredients so that it is a balanced weeknight dinner, ready in 30 mins. Most importantly, it is delicious!!

Ingredients

  • Vegetarian laksa paste (I use Tungsan brand) - 200gm
  • Hokkien round mee - 1 packet - 450g
  • Xiao Bai Cai - 2 packets (this recipe is heavy on the veg!)
  • Tau Kwa - 2 pieces cut into small cubes
  • Fried Tofu puffs - 1 packet cut into small cubes
  • Light coconut milk - 330ml - Ayam Brand
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the noodles by blanching them in hot water for 20secs. Drain the noodles and set aside
  2. Blanch the fried tofu puffs in hot water to remove excess oil and drain
  3. In a wok, fry your tau kwa in some oil with salt and pepper until golden on all sides. Remove and keep aside.
  4. In the same wok, add some oil, add your laksa paste, saute till fragrant. If you feel it is burning, add some water. Add your xiao bai cai and let it cook.
  5. Add back tau kaw and tofu puffs. Add coconut milk. Simmer
  6. After simmering for a few minutes, add the noodles. Simmer for another 5 minutes. Serve immediately!
